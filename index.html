Healthy Hummus

Homemade Hummus

Recipe by: Wholefoodscollective.com.au

Ingredients list heading

  • 1 tin chickpeas (approx 250g rinsed)
  • 2 tbsp tahini (hulled)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon (juice of)
  • 1/2 to 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Water as needed for desired consistency
  • Optional toppings: Flax seeds, sesame seeds, and fresh basil

Equipment list heading

  • Stick blender, regular blender, or food processor
  • Measuring spoons
  • Citrus juicer
  • Fine mesh strainer

Directions heading

  1. Place the rinsed chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and cumin into your blending device.
  2. Blend all ingredients thoroughly until smooth.
  3. Assess the consistency and add water only after the initial blend to reach your desired thickness.
  4. Alternatively, use more lemon juice or oil to thin the consistency instead of water.
  5. Transfer to a bowl and top with olive oil, a sprinkle of seeds, and a sprig of fresh basil.
  6. Store in the fridge for up to approximately one week.
